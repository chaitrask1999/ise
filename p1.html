What is Artificial Intelligence ?

In computer-science,aritificial intelligence ,sometimes called machine intelligence,is intelligence demonstrated by machines ,in contrast to natural intelligence displayed by humans.In other words, Computer-science defines AI research as the study of "intelligent agents":any device that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of successfully achieving its goals.
