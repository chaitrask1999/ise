What is Internet Of Things ?

The Internet of Things is a system of interrelated computing devices,mechanical and digital machines,objects,animals or people tha are provded with unique identifiers (UIDs) and the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human or human-to-computer interaction.
